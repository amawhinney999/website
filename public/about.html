About Don Bosco Hotel School

Our Mission

Don Bosco Hotel School provides hospitality training and education to disadvantaged youth, equipping them with practical skills and career opportunities in the tourism and service industries.

Students learning practical hospitality skills.

Our Story

Since 2007, we have combined a working training hotel with classroom learning — students learn by doing, supported by experienced instructors and community partners. Guests staying at the hotel directly support vocational training for disadvantaged young Cambodians.

Don Bosco Hotel and Training Centre in Sihanoukville.

Education & Training

Students rotate through reception, food & beverage, kitchen and housekeeping departments so they graduate with hands-on experience and confidence to join the workforce.

Professional training in classroom and hotel settings.

Technical & Trade Skills Beyond Hospitality

Beyond hospitality, the wider organisation offers training in welding, automotive, electronics and electrical systems — opening career paths for youth from low-income backgrounds.

Quick Facts

  • Established: 2007
  • Programmes: Hospitality, Culinary, Reception, Welding, Automotive, Electronics
  • Location: Sihanoukville, Cambodia
  • Focus: Education & empowerment for underprivileged Cambodian youth
