Our Services

Experience comfort, convenience, and Cambodian hospitality at Don Bosco Hotel.

Free Shuttle Service

Enjoy a complimentary daily shuttle lift to Otres Beach or the city centre of Sihanoukville. Departures at 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM every day.

Khmer Cooking Class

Join our authentic Khmer cooking class to discover the secrets of Cambodian cuisine. This is a paid experience led by our professional chefs — learn, cook, and taste your creations!

Room Features

Each room includes a private balcony or terrace, bathrobes, and complimentary WiFi for all guests — offering the perfect balance of comfort and relaxation.

Leisure Facilities

Relax in our year-round outdoor swimming pool, stay active in the fitness centre, or unwind in our lush garden and terrace. The on-site restaurant serves lunch daily, and we offer both bicycle parking and free private parking for guests.
